Powered Data Quality Monitoring

This is a chatbot application using ChatGPT that monitors and evaluates the quality of data in retail systems and databases. This chatbot will examine data attributes, identify potential anomalies or issues, and promptly notify relevant stakeholders, empowering proactive data governance and timely corrective actions. It will perform comprehensive checks for completeness, consistency, and adherence to predefined rules, generating alerts if any data quality problems like missing values, inconsistent formatting, or outliers are detected.
